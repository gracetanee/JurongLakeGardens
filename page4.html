Recommended Exploration Routes

Map of Jurong Lake Gardens

Below is the map of Jurong Lake Gardens for you to plan your visit here! You may also wish to check out the full brochure below or follow the two recommended routes.



Click here for the full brochure


  • Route 1: Family Outings

    • (6) The Canyon, (13) The Logs Trail, (14) Freshwater Swamp, (11) Butterfly Maze, (9) Forest Ramble, (4) Clusia Cove


    This route is perfect for bringing your children out on an adventure! Explore the key play spots in Jurong Lake Gardens such as Clusia Cove while also enjoying the beauty of Jurong Lake Gardens in the Butterfly Maze.

    Entrance: Near Tah Ching Road



  • Route 2: A Getaway with Nature

    • (25) Neram Streams, (22) Rasau Walk, (21) Grasslands, (20) The Lone Tree, (17) Alstonia Island


    This route is suited for nature lovers who are looking for an afternoon of relaxation. Enjoy the best scenery of Jurong Lake Gardens through areas such as Rasau Walk and Alstonia Island.

    Entrance: Near Tao Ching Road
